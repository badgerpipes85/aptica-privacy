1. Getting started

MAP Energy helps you monitor and understand connected energy systems, including Tesla Powerwall, solar generation, EV charging and future smart energy integrations.

Tesla Powerwall Solar monitoring EV charging

2. Installing MAP Energy

MAP Energy is available on Google Play for Android devices.

Download MAP Energy on Google Play

3. Account setup

After installing the app, create or sign into your MAP Energy account. Your account is used to securely link supported energy services and manage premium access.

  • Install MAP Energy
  • Open the app
  • Sign in or create an account
  • Connect supported energy services

4. Tesla connection

MAP Energy can connect to Tesla Energy services to retrieve supported Powerwall and energy site data.

Tesla connection is used for supported cloud-based data. Some advanced diagnostic features may also require local Powerwall pairing.

5. Powerwall local pairing

Local pairing allows MAP Energy to communicate with your Powerwall on your local network for advanced diagnostics.

What you may need

  • Your Powerwall gateway password
  • A phone connected to the same local network
  • Access to the Powerwall if a pairing toggle is required

Local pairing is intended to unlock richer Powerwall diagnostics, such as battery system data, gateway information and future advanced monitoring features.

6. Powerwall diagnostics

The Powerwall diagnostics area is designed to show useful technical information from your system.

Current diagnostic areas

  • Battery system information
  • System state of charge
  • Rated and actual system capacity where available
  • Frequency and voltage
  • Gateway host and DIN information
  • Raw diagnostic output for support

Some values depend on your Powerwall model, firmware version, local access and system configuration.

7. Premium access

Some MAP Energy features may require an active premium subscription or valid promotional access.

  • Premium status is checked inside the app
  • Promo codes are redeemed through Google Play
  • Premium access may need the app to be restarted after purchase or redemption

8. Troubleshooting

Powerwall cannot be found

Make sure your phone is connected to the same local network as your Powerwall.

Pairing does not complete

Follow the in-app pairing instructions carefully. Some systems may require a Powerwall toggle before pairing is confirmed.

Internal tester warning on Google Play

If Google Play says you are an internal tester, leave the testing programme and reinstall the production version from Google Play.

Premium does not show immediately

Close and reopen MAP Energy after redeeming a code or purchasing premium access.
