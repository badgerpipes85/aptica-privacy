MAP Energy Documentation

Guidance for setting up MAP Energy, connecting energy systems, using Tesla Powerwall diagnostics, and understanding the data shown in the app.

Getting Started

MAP Energy helps you monitor and understand your connected energy systems, including Tesla Powerwall, solar generation, EV charging and future smart home energy integrations.

Install MAP Energy

Download MAP Energy from Google Play, create an account, and sign in to begin connecting your energy devices.

Tesla Connection

Connect your Tesla account to allow MAP Energy to access supported Powerwall and Tesla energy data.

Powerwall Local Pairing

Local pairing enables advanced Powerwall diagnostics, including live local data and future battery health insights.

Powerwall Diagnostics

View system capacity, battery state of charge, gateway information, voltage, frequency and other diagnostic values.

Solar & EV Integrations

MAP Energy is being built to support solar monitoring, EV charging data and smart energy device integrations.

Troubleshooting

Find help for connection issues, pairing problems, subscription access and local Powerwall communication.
