2. What this policy covers

This Privacy Policy explains how Map Energy collects and processes data when you use:

  • the Map Energy iOS app
  • related backend services

3. Data we collect

a) Account authentication

  • Apple Sign-In identifier
  • Session tokens required to keep you signed in

We do not receive your Apple ID email address unless you explicitly allow it.

b) Energy service connections

If you connect external energy services we may store credentials required to access those services.

Tesla Energy
  • Tesla energy site ID
  • Energy system configuration
  • Encrypted OAuth tokens

We cannot access your Tesla password.

Octopus Energy
  • API key
  • Account number
  • Tariff and meter identifiers
myenergi / Zappi
  • Zappi hub serial number
  • Zappi API key

These credentials are encrypted at rest and used only to retrieve EV charging telemetry.

c) Device & app data

  • App generated device identifier
  • Push notification tokens
  • Minimal usage data required for functionality

d) Automation data

  • User created automation rules
  • Automation execution history
  • Timing and configuration metadata

e) Energy telemetry

Map Energy may retrieve operational energy telemetry such as:

  • Grid import and export power
  • Solar generation
  • Battery state of charge
  • Home energy consumption
  • EV charging power
  • EV charging state

This data does not include personal identity information such as names or addresses.

4. What we do not collect

  • Names
  • Home addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Payment information
  • Advertising identifiers

We do not sell personal data.

5. How we use data

  • Provide live energy views
  • Retrieve energy telemetry
  • Detect EV charging sessions
  • Run user configured automations
  • Send notifications you enable
  • Maintain security and reliability

6. Legal basis (UK GDPR)

  • Contractual necessity – providing app functionality
  • User consent – connecting third-party services
  • Legitimate interests – security and reliability

7. Data security

  • Sensitive credentials are encrypted at rest
  • Infrastructure is hosted on Cloudflare
  • Access is restricted and logged

8. Third-party services

  • Tesla Energy APIs
  • Octopus Energy APIs
  • myenergi Zappi APIs
  • Apple Sign-In
  • Apple Push Notification Service

9. International transfers

Some processing may occur outside the UK/EU where required by third-party services.
