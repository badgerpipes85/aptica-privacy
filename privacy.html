Privacy Policy

MAP Energy (the “App”) is operated by Aptica Ltd.

Last updated: January 2026

Summary: MAP Energy helps you view and manage energy information (including optional integrations such as Tesla Energy and Octopus Energy). We collect the minimum data needed to run the App, keep it secure, and provide the features you choose to enable.

1) Who we are

Controller: Aptica Ltd (“Aptica”, “we”, “us”, “our”) is the data controller for personal data processed through MAP Energy.

Contact: support@aptica.uk

2) What data we collect

A. Account and identifiers

  • Apple Sign-In identifiers (e.g., a stable user identifier provided by Apple).
  • Device identifiers used to link a device to your account/session for app functionality (e.g., an app-generated device ID).

B. Configuration and app data

  • Energy configuration data you enter or generate through the App (e.g., supplier settings, display preferences, tariff configuration, site configuration).
  • Automation configuration (if enabled), such as rules and schedules you set inside the App.
  • Operational metadata (e.g., timestamps like “last sync”, connection status, and basic diagnostic events).

C. Third-party integration data (only if you connect them)

  • Octopus Energy: your Octopus API key and account number (entered by you) and related account/tariff details retrieved from Octopus.
  • Tesla Energy (Owner API/local access): tokens/credentials used to access Tesla Energy data you authorise, and retrieved energy/telemetry data (e.g., battery state of charge, power flows) depending on your setup.
  • Tesla Fleet / Automations: if you enable automations, we store Tesla Fleet authorisation tokens on our backend to perform actions you request and to run automations you enable.

We do not collect special category data (e.g., health, biometrics) and we do not sell your personal data.

3) How we use your data

  • Provide app functionality (sign-in, storing settings, showing your energy data).
  • Enable integrations you choose (e.g., Octopus and Tesla connections).
  • Run automations you enable (including executing scheduled actions and keeping history of automation runs).
  • Maintain security and prevent abuse (e.g., session management, rate limiting, fraud/abuse detection).
  • Troubleshooting and support when you contact us (we use the information you provide to help resolve issues).

4) Legal bases (UK GDPR)

  • Contract: to provide the App and features you request.
  • Consent: for connecting optional third-party services and enabling automations.
  • Legitimate interests: to keep the App secure, reliable, and to prevent abuse (balanced against your rights).
  • Legal obligation: if required by applicable law (e.g., responding to lawful requests).

5) Sharing and third parties

We share data only as needed to provide the App:

  • Apple for Sign in with Apple authentication.
  • Octopus Energy (if connected) to fetch your authorised account and tariff data.
  • Tesla (if connected) to access Tesla Energy data and/or Fleet authorisation for automations.
  • Infrastructure providers used to host and operate our backend (e.g., serverless hosting, databases, and storage). These providers process data under our instructions.

We do not allow third parties to use your data for their own marketing.

6) Where data is stored and international transfers

Your data may be processed in the UK and other countries depending on the location of our infrastructure and third-party providers. Where international transfers occur, we use appropriate safeguards (such as contractual protections) as required by applicable law.

7) Data retention

  • Account and configuration data: kept while you use the App, and deleted or anonymised when no longer needed.
  • Integration tokens: stored only while the integration is enabled. If you disconnect an integration, we revoke/mark tokens as disconnected and remove stored secrets where applicable.
  • Automation history: retained to show you past automation runs and to help diagnose issues, then deleted or aggregated after a reasonable period.

Exact retention may vary depending on technical requirements, support needs, and legal obligations.

8) Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit (HTTPS) and access controls. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.

9) Your rights

You may have rights under UK data protection law, including:

  • Access to your personal data
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion (in certain cases)
  • Restriction or objection to processing (in certain cases)
  • Data portability (where applicable)
  • Withdraw consent (for processing based on consent)

To exercise rights, email support@aptica.uk. We may need to verify your identity.

10) How to disconnect integrations / delete data

  • Disconnect Tesla / Octopus: use the App settings to disconnect the integration. This disables access and updates our stored authorisation state.
  • Delete your account/data: email support@aptica.uk with the subject Data Deletion Request. Include the email used with Apple Sign-In (or your Apple relay email if used).

11) Children

MAP Energy is not intended for children and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

12) Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will change the “Last updated” date above and, where appropriate, provide additional notice in the App.

13) Complaints

If you have concerns, please contact us first at support@aptica.uk. You also have the right to complain to the UK Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO).
