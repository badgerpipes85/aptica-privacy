Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Aptica Ltd ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal data when you use the Aptica mobile application.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Data is shared only with third-party services you explicitly connect (such as Tesla or Octopus Energy).

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data using modern security practices.

Your Rights

You may disconnect third-party services at any time and request deletion of your data by contacting us.
